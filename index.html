Embark on a captivating journey through the vast and wild world of Hyrule. In "The Legend of Zelda: Breath of the Wild," players are given unprecedented freedom to explore at their own pace, uncovering hidden secrets and overcoming challenges in a beautifully crafted open world.

"Animal Crossing: New Horizons" offers a unique escape to a deserted island where creativity and friendship flourish. Build your community from scratch, explore seasonal activities, and enjoy the simple pleasures of island life in this engaging, open-ended adventure.

Join Mario as he journeys across various worlds on his hat-shaped ship, the Odyssey, in "Super Mario Odyssey." This globe-trotting adventure is filled with new surprises, kingdoms to explore, and innovative gameplay that leverages Mario's new abilities like never before.
